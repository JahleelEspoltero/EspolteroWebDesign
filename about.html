Background

We are one of the most trusted Jersey makers in the Philippines, our unique designs have been very well known for years now and we aim to continue that reputation. Located at Dr Arcadio Santos Ave, Parañaque, Metro Manila, MNL Kingpin South was established in 2021 and has been the favorite go-to sportswear brand of local celebrities and players since. We create and innovate; let us help you enhance your next team uniform with premium quality and great customer service.
